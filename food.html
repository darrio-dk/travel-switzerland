Fondue

Swiss dish of melted cheese is made from several types of cheese, one variety is undesirable to use. It is important that the product dissolves well. Mostly Gruyere, Vazheran, Emmental. This cheese fondue is prepared with cherry kirsha. It is a strong alcoholic drink that was previously used only for medicinal purposes. But now it is increasingly replaced with white wine, gin, sometimes with champagne.

Basel-style roasted flour soup

A true Basel citizen enjoys this fortifying soup made from flour roasted brown, onions, butter, and broth.

Raclette

Another famous cheese dish that the Swiss people like to make is Raclette. A whole piece of cheese is slightly heated over the fire until one part of it starts to melt. That softened part of the cheese is then cut off and served with pickled vegetables, fat-sizzling fries and bread.

Tartiflette

A bit of French cuisine which was conceived near French-Swiss border. A very tasty, hearty casserole is made from potatoes, mature soft reblochon cheese, sour cream and bacon.
